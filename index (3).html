About Me

I'm Mishika — a dual-degree student at IIM Bangalore and St. Joseph's College of Law. I study business and law at the same time, not because it's easy, but because I genuinely find both fascinating and I've never been great at picking just one thing.

I care about how conflicts get resolved, how policy gets made, and how businesses navigate complexity. Somewhere in that overlap is where I want to build my career.

I'm early in that journey — curious, open, and always looking for the next thing to learn. If there's a room where interesting decisions are being made, I want to be in it — and eventually, I want to help shape what gets decided.
